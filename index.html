12comply

12comply is a Task Reminder Application for Compliance purposes. Our platform provides a comprehensive set of recurrent reminders based on GDPR and other legal regulations, developed in collaboration with data protection specialists. Moreover, 12comply facilitates the planning and assignment of recurrent office tasks to employees. By streamlining task management processes, our platform enhances efficiency and productivity within your organization.
